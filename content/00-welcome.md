# Welcome, Kat 🧡

A guide that grows with you — from your first prompt to your first paying customer.

You don't read this from start to finish. You **build the projects** as you go. Each chapter ends with something real, something you can show people, something on the internet.

The whole game is one loop: **describe → look at it → describe what's wrong → repeat.** That's it. Lovable and Claude do the typing. You do the thinking.

## What you already have

You're not starting from zero. You already have:

- A **GitHub** account
- A **Supabase** account  
- **Claude Code** installed
- A laptop that works
- Curiosity

That's more than 95% of people who say *"someday I'll learn to code."* The hard part — the deciding-to-start part — you've already done.

## How this site works

This guide lives in a repository on GitHub. When your dad (or you) edit a chapter and push it, this page updates in about 30 seconds. So the guide can keep growing as you do.

There's a small "Edit this chapter on GitHub" link in the sidebar — that's where I'd add notes, fixes, and new ideas as you go.

## A note before you begin

There will be moments when something breaks and you'll feel stupid. That feeling is the actual job. Every developer in the world — including the ones at Anthropic and Lovable — feels it weekly. The skill isn't avoiding that feeling. The skill is staying in your chair five more minutes.

Now turn the page. We're starting with the smallest possible website.
